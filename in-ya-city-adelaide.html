TAILORING YOUR COACHING FOCUS

For the first time, Chuby Dice brought his true Jamaican Dancehall to Adelaide.

When: Sun 15 Feb, 5:30–7PM
Where: 139 Bradly Grove, Mitchell Park, Room D
Price: $30

This session brought fulla Yard vibes as Chuby carried "Dancehall to di World" to the City of Churches.

Participants explored the roots & enduring legacy of Jamaican Dancehall, with a focus on pioneers of the style & Black Dice crew's famous party moves.

The workshop gave Adelaide dancers direct connection to Chuby's practice and a chance to carry these moves forward in their own way.
