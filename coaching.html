What to expect

TAILORING YOUR COACHING FOCUS

Professional 1:1 Coaching with Chuby offers structured dance & Dancehall instruction from Chuby as a pioneering creator & cross-country performer.

1:1 coaching is fully individualised and can include tailoring a program based on your levels & goals. These can be technique, performance, teaching, Dancehall moves & execution as well as research-based inquiry. Contact Chuby to develop sequenced programs, focused on your longer term growth.

Book a 1:1 session or a consultation with Chuby to design your program.

Coaching programs can focus on:

Dancehall foundations & movement quality

Build your core Dancehall technique from the ground up. Covers bounce, groove patterns, isolations, footwork and body control — all rooted in authentic Jamaican movement practice and tailored to your current level.

Sub-genres & Dancehall periods

Explore the eras and styles that define Dancehall — from early 90s foundation through mid-skool to contemporary new school. Learn how each period shaped movement vocabulary, musical selections and cultural identity.

Clash techniques versus party Dancehall

Understand the difference between competitive battle-ready movement and social party Dancehall. Develop the precision, confidence and spontaneous expression needed for both contexts.

Choreography & musicality

Sharpen your ability to hear and move with the music. Covers musical phrasing, timing, rhythmic accents and how to build choreographic sequences that connect movement to selection.

Dancehall history, theory & contemporary practice

Go deeper into the cultural, social and political roots of Dancehall. Covers key pioneers, the sound system tradition, Jamaican street culture and how contemporary Dancehall continues to evolve globally.
